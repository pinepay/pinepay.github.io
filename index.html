The future of Payments

Pine is a mobile payment application platform that empower merchants and rewards users

Every user has a smartphone, yet carrying a physical wallet to pay merchants is needed. Merchants do not obtain loyalty when a customer pays with cards and are unable to reward users.

Pine offers merchants digital payment collection with lower transaction fees; and users experience ultra-convenience with a digital wallet integrated with personalized rewards.

The Pine app will soon be available on both iOS & Android with the following features:

  1. Users and Merchants can register profiles, while Pine verifies and secures

  2. The Pine app facilitates wallet loading and withdrawals from bank accounts and digital wallet transfers

  3. Merchants offer in-app tailored rewards to recommended Pine users
