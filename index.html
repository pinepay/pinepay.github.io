The Future of Payments

Pine Pay is a mobile payment application platform that empowers merchants and rewards users

Everyone has a smartphone, yet we still carry wallets. Merchants have powerful processors in their pockets, yet must still use outdated card machine systems, with no digital relationship to their customers.

Pine Pay embraces the future of mobile. We solve payment problems for merchants and users with an app-driven, all-mobile, data-driven payment ecosystem. We offer merchants a digital payment collection with lower transaction fees; and users experience ultra-convenience with a digital wallet integrated with personalized rewards.
